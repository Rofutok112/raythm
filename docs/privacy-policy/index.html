Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the rhythm game "raythm", its related online features, raythm-Server, and this site published on GitHub Pages.

If users access external websites, external services, or external links included in posted content, the handling of information by those services is governed by their own policies.

Information We Collect

raythm may collect or store the following information within the scope necessary to provide its features.

  • Account information: email address, display name, password, email verification status, and verification code results
  • Authentication information: access tokens, refresh tokens, and trusted device tokens
  • Ranking information: chart ID, song ID, play time, submission time, note judgement results, offset values for each judgement, score, accuracy, max combo, clear rank, and full-combo status
  • Community upload information: song title, artist name, BPM, audio files, jacket images, external links, chart files, difficulty names, chart author names, and visibility status
  • Download and catalog information: request information needed to list, view, and download songs, charts, and related files
  • Information automatically sent during communication: IP address, User-Agent, access time, requested URL, communication error information, and similar information commonly recorded for server operations
  • Locally stored information: settings, local rankings, imported songs, charts, MVs, login sessions, and mappings between local content and online content

This site is distributed as a static site through GitHub Pages. This site does not provide its own input forms or independent analytics, but GitHub may process access-related information.

Purposes of Use

Collected information is used for the following purposes.

  • To provide account registration, login, logout, email verification, session restoration, and account deletion
  • To submit, verify, display, and manage online rankings and personal bests
  • To verify official chart integrity, retrieve scoring rules, and help prevent ranking abuse
  • To upload, update, delete, publish, and download community songs and charts
  • To display uploaded content and ranking history in profile views
  • To investigate defects, improve security, prevent misuse, and improve the service
  • To respond to inquiries and provide important notices or policy change notices

Information That May Be Public

When users use online features, the following information may be visible to other users.

  • Ranking information such as display name, placement, score, accuracy, max combo, clear rank, full-combo status, and play time
  • Metadata, audio files, jacket images, chart files, external links, and uploader-related information for songs and charts posted with public visibility

Email addresses, passwords, and authentication tokens are not made public for ranking or catalog display.

Third-Party Sharing and External Services

raythm does not provide personal information to third parties except when required by law, with user consent, or when information must be handled by service providers or hosting providers within the scope necessary for service operation.

This site is hosted through GitHub Pages. Please refer to GitHub's privacy-related documents for information about GitHub's handling of data.

Retention Period

Account information, ranking information, and uploaded content are retained for as long as necessary to provide the service, or until the user requests deletion or performs a deletion action. When an account is deleted, related information will be deleted or de-identified except where retention is necessary for service operation.

Locally stored information is stored on the user's device. It may be deleted by uninstalling the application, deleting local data on the device, or using deletion features in the game where available.

Security

raythm strives to take necessary and appropriate measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or leakage of handled information. Some locally stored information, such as authentication information and local rankings, may be stored using device-side protection features.

However, no internet communication or device storage can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure. Users should avoid reusing passwords and should manage their devices and accounts appropriately.

User Controls and Requests

Users may be able to log out, delete their account, and delete community songs or charts they uploaded through in-game features.

If you wish to request access, correction, deletion, suspension of use, or similar handling of your information, please contact us using the contact information below. We may ask you to provide information necessary to verify your identity.

Use by Minors

Users who are minors should obtain consent from a parent or guardian before using online features, registering an account, or posting community content.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in response to feature additions, legal changes, or changes in operation policy. If there are important changes, we will provide notice through this site, in-game notices, or another appropriate method.

Contact

For inquiries about this Privacy Policy or the handling of personal information, please contact us through the following channel.

  • GitHub Issues: https://github.com/Rofutok112/raythm/issues. This is a public location, so do not include email addresses, authentication information, or other personal information.
  • Contact for inquiries that include personal information: raythm.game@gmail.com
